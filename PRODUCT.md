# Product

## Register

product

## Users
People who want a lightweight browser instrument for quick play, sound exploration, or casual music sketching. They interact directly on desktop or touch devices and need the interface to respond immediately with minimal setup.

## Product Purpose
This product provides a compact synthesizer that runs in the browser, lets users play notes with a keyboard or pointer, switch synth engines, and stack effects in real time. Success means the instrument feels fast, playable, and easy to understand without extra instruction.

## Brand Personality
Direct, studio-inspired, responsive. The interface should feel focused and technical without becoming dense or intimidating.

## Anti-references
Avoid toy-like music app styling, cluttered DAW complexity, and marketing-heavy landing page patterns that compete with the act of playing. Avoid decorative copy that repeats what the controls already communicate.

## Design Principles
Keep the instrument in immediate reach.
Favor clear control grouping over extra explanation.
Use visual feedback to confirm audio state and note activity.
Preserve a compact layout that works for mouse, touch, and keyboard play.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Target WCAG AA contrast for text and controls. Ensure the instrument remains usable with keyboard input, visible focus states, and reduced reliance on explanatory copy so the control labels carry the interaction model.
